Admissions & Enrollment

Join Saint Laurent de Gaseke and launch your career in technical and vocational excellence

Eligibility Requirements

📚 Academic Background

Minimum completion of lower secondary education (S2/Form 2) or equivalent. Students with strong mathematics and science grades are preferred.

🎯 Age & Maturity

Must be at least 16 years old at time of enrollment. Demonstrated commitment to technical learning and career development.

📋 Documentation

Original secondary school certificate or transcript, birth certificate, national ID/passport, and passport-sized photos (4 copies).

Application Process

Follow these steps to apply for admission to Saint Laurent de Gaseke:

Step 1: Submit Application

Complete the application form available at the admissions office or online portal. Include all required documents and pay the application fee.

Step 2: Entrance Exam

Take the entrance examination covering Mathematics, English, and General Knowledge. The exam assesses your readiness for technical studies.

Step 3: Interview

Participate in a personal interview with admissions committee. Discuss your interests, career goals, and why you choose our school.

Step 4: Admission Decision

Receive your admission decision via email or letter. Successful applicants proceed to registration and orientation.
